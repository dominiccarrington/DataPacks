Utilities

These datapacks can be dropped into any world and do useful things that might help you or your server. These datapacks should be compatible with other and any other datapacks on your world.

Objective Displayed

Since you can only show one objective under your name, this datapack rotates it every 30 seconds. Simply created so you can add any other objective under the name by following suit.
